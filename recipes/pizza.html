Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a delicious pizza recipe and easy to follow

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons white pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the garlic and oregano until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in the basil, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. In a medium saucepan, combine the olive oil, sugar, garlic, salt, and pepper; cook over medium heat until the sugar dissolves, about 2 minutes.
  6. Add the water, and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat to low and simmer until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  8. Stir in the flour and heat through.
  9. Combine the pizza sauce, cheese, and remaining ingredients.
  10. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the bottom of a 9-inch pie plate.
  11. Top with the cheese mixture.
  12. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown, about 15 minutes.
