Couscous Recipe

Description

This is a delicious couscous recipe and easy to follow

This recipe is made with mutton and chicken, but you can easily change the meats for lamb and/or merguez. I make this often and my family loves it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mutton
  • 1 pound chicken
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 onions
  • 1/2 cup courgettes
  • 1/2 cup red pepper
  • 1/2 cup green pepper
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Heat up oil in a large pan
  2. Add mutton and chicken
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Add onions
  5. Add courgettes, red and green pepper
  6. Add water
  7. Cook for 30 minutes
  8. Add olive oil
